# Infographic
A Infographic about me, Karla Landsberg. This includes details about things I care about and what I am working towards

## Logo Explanation
The three wavy lines in the back symbolises the movements in rhythm I experience in my daily life as a professional ballerina.
A dragonfly is a common symbol of growth and agility. I have grown so much professionally and personally in the past few years and agility has been an essential skill on my journey.
The dragonfly is also a personal symbol of my grandmother, which also higlighths its importance in my logo.
I used the same colour scheme in my logo and in my infographic to ensure that the two are interconnected and do not stand alone. I also incorporated dragonfly symbols throughout my infographic in order to further strengthen that connection.

## Colour Scheme
The colours I chose for this infographic was gold and blue. Blue is the classic colour of calm and serenity, which is exactly the theme I was going for. I used gold ,because I love sunshine immensely and gold is one of the colours that represents sunshine. Gold also makes me think of laughter and joy, which is two abstract verbs that I feel are very important in life.

## Reflection
Designing the Infographic was harder than I anticipated. It challenged me to think critically about who I am and what I care about. I struggled to decide how my logo should look and what elements I wanted to include. I had multiple rough drafts of logos and none felt right,even the logo I have know does not quite feel like me. I think this design shows a bit about my perfectionism and my personality.
